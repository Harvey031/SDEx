SD Gundam

EX-STANDARD

SD Gundam EX-Standard (SDEX) (SDガンダム エクスタンダード SD Gandamu
Ekusu Sutandādo?) is a line of Super Deformed Gunpla kits of the Mobile
Suits from the Gundam metaseries. Initially released exclusively for sale outside
of Japan in late 2015, the line was introduced in Japan in the Summer of 2016.
Each kit has a gimmick wherein their weapons and accessories can be used
with High Grade 1/144 Gunpla kits.

The SD Gundam EX-Standard line was discontinued in 2017 and replaced by the SD Gundam Cross Silhouette line. The
SDEX RX-78-2 Gundam  was relaunched with Hello Kitty in February 2020 to commemorate the 40th anniversary of
Gundam and the 45th anniversary of Hello Kitty. The line was continued in late 2020 with the releases of RX-93 ν
Gundam and MSN-04 Sazabi.

Reissues of the older kits from 2020 onwards will feature the blue Bandai Spirits logo.
